Questions

FREQUENTLY
ASKED.

You're 17. How do I know the quality will be professional?
My age is just a number — my work speaks for itself. Every site in my portfolio was delivered to a paying client who saw real, measurable results. I've spent years studying conversion design, web performance, and modern development. You're not paying for seniority, you're paying for results.
How does the 2-week delivery actually work?
Week 1: Discovery call, strategy, and design prototype. You review and approve the design. Week 2: Full development, testing, SEO setup, and launch. The only thing that delays this is late feedback from your side — which is why we keep the process structured and clear.
What if I don't like the design?
You approve the design before we build. We present the full visual prototype first, and you can request revisions until it's right. We don't start coding until you're happy. This eliminates surprises at the end.
Can you help with content and copywriting too?
Yes — copywriting is included in the Growth and Premium plans. Good copy is what separates a website that looks good from one that converts. We write persuasive, SEO-friendly content tailored to your specific audience in Sri Lanka.
Do you offer payment in LKR?
Absolutely. All projects can be quoted and paid in Sri Lankan Rupees. We also offer a 50% upfront / 50% on delivery payment structure so there's no risk on your end.
What happens after the site launches?
Depending on your plan, you get 30–60 days of free post-launch support. That covers bug fixes, small content updates, and any tweaks you need. After that, we offer affordable monthly maintenance packages so your site stays fast and up-to-date.
