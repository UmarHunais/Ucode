PRICING
FAQ.

Why no exact fixed prices?
Every business is different. A restaurant in Colombo 7 has different needs than a construction company in Galle. The "starting from" model means we scope the project together — you never pay for things you don't need, and we never under-deliver to hit an arbitrary number.
Are there any hidden fees after the project ends?
None. The only ongoing costs are third-party: domain renewal (roughly LKR 3,000–5,000/yr) and hosting (roughly LKR 2,500–6,000/yr) — which you pay directly to the provider. We don't mark these up or take a cut. Transparency is non-negotiable.
Can I upgrade my plan later?
Yes. Many clients start on Starter and expand to Growth as their business grows. We treat your site as a living asset, and we're happy to add pages, features, or a full redesign down the line. Existing clients also get priority scheduling.
What if I only have a rough idea of what I want?
That's perfectly fine — and honestly, most clients come with just an idea. The strategy call is specifically designed to draw out what you actually need. We'll define the scope, pages, and goals together before any money changes hands.
Do I need to provide content and images?
Growth and Authority plans include copywriting, so you don't need to provide text. For images, we work with licensed stock photography if you don't have brand photography yet. If you have your own photos, even better — we'll use those. Starter clients will need to provide their own copy.
Is there a refund policy?
You approve the design prototype before we build. If you're unhappy before development begins, we'll revise until it's right. The 50/50 structure protects both parties — but in practice, we've never had a client who wasn't satisfied. The work speaks for itself.
